# **Web Style Guide: Henka Consulting**

**Version 2.2 \- March 2026** **Concept:** "Technical Engineering of Change"

**Visual Language:** Swiss Architectural Style (International Typographic Style)

## **1\. Executive Summary**

This guide transitions Henka from traditional consultancy aesthetics to a high-precision **Architectural & Engineering** visual framework. By utilizing a "Blueprint" aesthetic, we communicate that organizational change is not a chaotic event, but a structured, safe, and engineered process. The core pillars are **Technical Rigor**, **Structural Safety**, and **Boutique Modernity**.

## **2\. The Architectural Grid System**

The layout is governed by an infinite coordinate system, transforming the viewport into a technical map.

### **2.1 The Infinite 1px Grid (Subtle Implementation)**

* **Infrastructure:** The interface is divided by continuous **1px solid lines**.  
* **Line Color:** Use \#0b334c with **10% to 15% Opacity** (or a flat \#E2E8F0). The goal is for the lines to be felt rather than seen, providing a subtle structural skeleton.  
* **Markers (+):** Every intersection of the grid must be marked with a **Navy Bold "+" symbol** using \#0b334c at **100% Opacity**. These markers act as the primary visual anchors of the coordinate system.  
* **Border Radius:** Strictly **0px (Sharp Corners)** for all interactive and structural elements.

### **2.2 Alignment & Axis**

* **Master Axis:** Strict **Left-Alignment**. All content adheres to the vertical lines of the grid.  
* **Centering:** Strictly avoid centered elements to maintain the efficiency and directness of a technical schematic.

## **4\. Typography: Massive Hierarchy**

We use **Open Sans** to maintain a neutral, professional engineering feel.

### **4.1 Typographic Scale**

* **H1 (Impact Statements):** 72px \- 96px | **Bold (700)** | Color: \#0b334c.  
  * *Usage:* Massive, authoritative statements aligned to the left grid axis.  
* **Technical Labels:** 12px | **Regular (400)** | Color: \#0b334c or \#82bd23.  
  * *Format:* Enclosed in square brackets. Ej: \[01\] THE CHALLENGE.  
* **Body Text:** 16px | **Regular (400)** | Color: \#0b334c.  
  * *Line-height:* 1.6 (Strictly aligned to the 8pt baseline).

## **5\. UI Components & Iconography**

### **5.1 Technical Iconography**

* **Style:** Precision Line Art. Icons must look like engineering schematics.  
* **Stroke:** 1px consistent width.  
* **Color:** \#84bd2a (brand-action).

### **5.2 Buttons (Structural Blocks)**

* **Primary CTA:** Solid Rectangle | \#84bd2a background | White Text | **0px Radius**.  
* **Ghost CTA:** 1px Navy Border (\#0b334c) | Navy Text | **0px Radius**.  
* **Hover Behavior:** Instant state change (No soft transitions). Use a "Solid Offset Shadow" effect (1px) to maintain the mechanical feel.

### **5.3 Card Modules**

* Cards are defined solely by the **1px Subtle Grid Lines**. Avoid containers with shadows; let the grid define the boundaries of information.

## **6\. Spacing (The 8pt Engineering System)**

All spacing is calculated based on an 8pt base unit to ensure mathematical harmony.

* **Module Scale:** 8, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 80, 128\.  
* **Internal Padding:** Always ensure the "+" markers are visible and not obscured by content.

## **7\. Imagery: Macro Textures**

* **Treatment:** Use high-quality macro textures (scales, urban structures) with a Navy Overlay.  
* **Placement:** Images should be "contained" within the grid modules, respecting the 1px dividers.

## **8\. Accessibility & Compliance**

* **Contrast:** Ensure that the \#0b334c text remains legible against the \#f4f6f9 background.  
* **Visual Guides:** The green accent \#84bd2a must be used sparingly to guide the user's attention to primary action points.
